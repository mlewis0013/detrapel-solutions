The Difference

Why Settle for Ordinary When Your Brand Deserves Extraordinary?

Traditional Aerosols

  • Toxic chemical propellants harmful to users and the environment
  • Wasteful — up to 15% of product left in the can, unusable
  • Opaque aluminum can — customers can't see the product inside
  • Limited spray angles — struggle to use upside down

Airopack Technology

  • Pure air propellant — no toxic chemicals, safe for everyone
  • Full evacuation — piston design empties every last drop
  • Fully transparent — showcase your formula's beauty
  • 360° spray — works perfectly at any angle
Advantages

Why Airopack Wins

Six reasons beauty brands across North America are making the switch to air-powered packaging.

Detrapel Technology

Air-powered. Zero toxic propellants.

Healthy & Safe

Pure air propellant means zero exposure to toxic chemicals. Your customers get a clean, safe experience with every spray — no compromises on wellbeing.

Sustainable by Design

Made from recycled PET with no harmful propellants. It's packaging your customers can feel good about — and that helps your brand stand for something bigger.

360° Spray

The innovative piston system sprays consistently at any angle. Whether upright, sideways, or upside down, the performance never drops.

Continuous Performance

No pressure loss from first spray to last. The air-powered piston maintains constant, reliable output so your product performs exactly as designed.

Fully Transparent

Clear recycled PET lets your product shine. Customers see exactly what they're getting — color, texture, quality — building trust before they even spray.

Full Evacuation

The rising piston pushes every drop of product out. No waste, no shaking, no frustration — just 100% of your formula delivered to your customer.
