The Difference

Why Settle for Ordinary When Your Brand Deserves Extraordinary?

Traditional Aerosols

  • Toxic chemical propellants harmful to users and the environment
  • Wasteful — up to 15% of product left in the can, unusable
  • Opaque aluminum can — customers can't see the product inside
  • Limited spray angles — struggle to use upside down

Airopack Technology

  • Pure air propellant — no toxic chemicals, safe for everyone
  • Full evacuation — piston design empties every last drop
  • Fully transparent — showcase your formula's beauty
  • 360° spray — works perfectly at any angle
